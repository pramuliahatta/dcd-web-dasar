Timeline

  • 1902 - Born in Bukittinggi, West Sumatra.
  • 1917 - Joined Jong Sumatranen Bond and chosen as the treasurer of the organization.
  • 1919 - Went to Batavia to study at Hogere burgerschool.
  • 1922 - Went to Netherlands to study at Erasmus University Rotterdam and joined Indische Vereeniging. The organization later change its name to Perhimpoenan Indonesia.
  • 1926 - Become the chairman of Perhimpoenan Indonesia.
  • 1927 - Arrested by the Dutch authorities on charges of involvement in the rebellion in Indonesia.
  • 1929 - Released from the prison.
  • 1932 - Finishes university, returned to Indonesia and became the chairman of the New PNI.
  • 1933 - Wrote many articles including "Soekarno is Arrested", "The Tragedy of Soekarno", and "The Stance of a Leader"
  • 1934 - Jailed in Glodok. He wrote a book entitled "The Economical Crisis and Capitalism" while in jail.
  • 1935 - Exiled to Digul. He continue to writes some article in newspapers while being exiled.
  • 1937 - He was transfered to Bandaneira.
  • 1942 - Transfered to Sukabumi and later transfered again to Jakarta.
  • 1945 - Accompanying Soekarno to proclaimed Indonesia's independence after many preparation was done.
  • 1956 - Resigned from vice presidency for several reasons.
  • 1980 - Dies at the age of 77
